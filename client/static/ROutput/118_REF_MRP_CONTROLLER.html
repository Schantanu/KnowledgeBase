Info

The REF_MRP_CONTROLLER has information about the current person responsible for a Commodity in the different areas of the Supply Chain. The areas being:

  • Commodity Planners - Responsible for identifying needs and issues, expedite the activities to maintain optimum material levels, facilitate commodity transfer, and improve stock management processes.

  • Material Managers - Responsible for procuring items are aligning with contractual terms and conditions, benchmarking with standard lead-times, and ensure delivery precision key performance indicators support successful end customer deployment satisfaction.

  • Commodity Buyer

  • Sourcing Prime - Responsible for developing the sources of supply for Products and Services for stakeholders across the region. They provide an appropriate balance of cost, quality, and service from the suppliers.

  • PPM Manager
